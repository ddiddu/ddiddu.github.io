Jisu Kim

MS @ GT Interactive Computing

I am a graduating master's student in Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech, advised by Richmond Wong and Ashok Goel. During my undergraduate studies at KAIST School of Computing and KAIST College of Business, I was advised by Juho Kim. My research interests lie in responsible AI and human-AI interaction, with a focus on designing and building AI that augments diverse and context-sensitive human values such as creativity, critical thinking, and cultural nuance. I am especially interested in (1) understanding how AI-assisted environments influence human sensemaking, (2) developing AI that fosters human competence and agency, and moving forward, (3) incorporating diverse cultural and linguistic perspectives to build more inclusive AI. My background in ML and DS, gained through industry internships at Tesla, Samsung SDS, and Samsung Research, equips me with the technical skills that support these research goals. I am actively exploring research assistant positions as I prepare to graduate in July 2025 with plans to pursue a PhD thereafter. Feel free to reach out if you are interested in collaborating!
