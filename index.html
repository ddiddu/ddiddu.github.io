Hi there! I’m an AI Engineer at FlexTecs, and I’m applying to PhD programs.

I recently completed my master’s at Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech, where I was fortunate to be advised by Ashok Goel and Richmond Wong. I also collaborated closely with Christopher J. MacLellan and Brian Magerko. I hold a double major in KAIST School of Computing and KAIST College of Business, where I was advised by Juho Kim.

My research aims to design AI systems that expand—rather than replace—human values, skills, and agency. I focus on (1) understanding how people perform and adapt in AI-assisted environments, and (2) developing AI systems that deliberately cultivate human thinking, enabling people to become more reflective, self-directed thinkers.

During my master’s and bachelor’s studies, I gained industry experience as an AI Engineer/Data Scientist at Tesla (2024), Samsung SDS (2021), and Samsung Research (2020).

My master’s research was supported by a Georgia Tech Graduate Research Assistant Scholarship. My bachelor's study was fully supported by the Korea Student Aid Foundation's National Scholarship.

Feel free to reach out if you're interested in talking about research, collaboration, or just want to chat!
